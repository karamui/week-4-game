Instructions

Select your hero and choose an ally to help you defeat The Empire!

Together you will face an enemy of your choosing. Your strength grows with each attack you make, so choose your opponents wisely and don't be disheartened when your enemy strikes back! Your rebellion succeeds when you have defeated all enemies.
